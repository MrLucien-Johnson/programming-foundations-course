## 💬 Feedback Review Checklist

### When Collecting Feedback

**Ask Specific Questions:**
- [ ] "How was your experience getting started?"
- [ ] "What did you find most helpful?"
- [ ] "What was confusing or frustrating?"
- [ ] "Did you complete the portfolio project?"
- [ ] "Would you recommend this to others?"

### When Reviewing Feedback

**Look For Patterns:**
- [ ] Common points of confusion
- [ ] Repeated positive feedback
- [ ] Suggestions that make sense
- [ ] Issues that need fixing

**Prioritize:**
- [ ] Critical issues (things that prevent learning)
- [ ] Common confusion points
- [ ] Easy wins (quick fixes)
- [ ] Nice-to-haves (future improvements)

**Take Action:**
- [ ] Fix critical issues immediately
- [ ] Clarify confusing sections
- [ ] Update materials based on feedback
- [ ] Thank people for their input

## 💡 Launch Day Mindset

### Do's ✅

- **Stay calm** - This is a soft launch, not a make-or-break moment
- **Be helpful** - Answer questions, provide support
- **Be grateful** - Thank people for interest and feedback
- **Stay grounded** - Focus on value delivered, not sales numbers
- **Celebrate small wins** - First interest, first purchase, first completion

### Don'ts ❌

- **Don't obsess** - Check sales once or twice, not constantly
- **Don't compare** - Your launch is unique, don't compare to others
- **Don't panic** - Low sales on day 1 is normal
- **Don't oversell** - Be helpful, not pushy
- **Don't take feedback personally** - Use it to improve

---

## 🆘 If Things Don't Go as Planned

### Low Interest?

**Check:**
- Are you sharing with the right people?
- Is your messaging clear?
- Are you reaching enough people?
- Is the course solving a real problem?

**Action:**
- Adjust messaging
- Try different channels
- Ask for honest feedback
- Be patient - soft launches are slow

### Technical Issues?

**Action:**
- Fix immediately
- Communicate transparently
- Apologize if needed
- Learn and prevent future issues

### Negative Feedback?

**Action:**
- Don't take it personally
- Look for valid points
- Fix what you can
- Thank them for honesty
- Use it to improve

**Remember:** Soft launch is about learning. Issues are learning opportunities.
