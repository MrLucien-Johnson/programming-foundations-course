## 💡 Message Writing Tips

### Do's ✅

- **Be personal** - Use your own voice
- **Be honest** - Don't oversell
- **Be helpful** - Focus on solving problems
- **Be clear** - Who it's for, what it includes
- **Be low-pressure** - No fake urgency

### Don'ts ❌

- **Don't hype** - Avoid "revolutionary," "game-changing"
- **Don't create fake urgency** - "Only 24 hours left!" (unless true)
- **Don't oversell** - Be realistic about outcomes
- **Don't spam** - Respect communities and people's time
- **Don't be pushy** - "No pressure" means no pressure

## 🎯 Message Customization Checklist

Before sending, make sure you've:

- [ ] Added your personal voice/tone
- [ ] Included specific details about your course
- [ ] Made it relevant to the recipient
- [ ] Removed any generic/hyped language
- [ ] Added your link/contact info
- [ ] Kept it concise and clear
- [ ] Made it helpful, not salesy
