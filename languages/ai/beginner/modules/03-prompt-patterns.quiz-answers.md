# Prompt Patterns Quiz Answers

## Question 1: Few-shot examples are used to:
**Answer: A** - Show the model the desired output

**Explanation:** Examples show the model what good output looks like.

---

## Question 2: Role prompting helps by:
**Answer: A** - Setting context and behavior

**Explanation:** Roles guide the tone and scope of the response.

---

## Question 3: A checklist prompt pattern is best for:
**Answer: A** - Ensuring all required steps are covered

**Explanation:** Checklists make outputs complete and consistent.
