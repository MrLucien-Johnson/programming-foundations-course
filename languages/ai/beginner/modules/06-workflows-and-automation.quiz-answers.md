# Workflows & Automation Quiz Answers

## Question 1: A prompt workflow is:
**Answer: A** - Multiple prompt steps connected together

**Explanation:** Workflows chain prompts into repeatable steps.

---

## Question 2: Why use prompt templates?
**Answer: A** - To reuse consistent instructions

**Explanation:** Templates keep prompts consistent and reusable.

---

## Question 3: A good handoff step includes:
**Answer: A** - Clear inputs and outputs

**Explanation:** Clear handoffs reduce mistakes between steps.
