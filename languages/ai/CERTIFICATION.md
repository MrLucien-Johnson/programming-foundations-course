# AI Certification Guide

This guide defines the requirements and evidence for AI certifications.

## Certified Beginner

### Required modules
- [AI Foundations (Beginner)](beginner/modules/foundations.md)

### Required projects
- [Prompt Playbook Project](beginner/projects/prompt-playbook.md)

### Evidence required
- evaluation harness evidence
- safety checklist
- cost and latency plan
- fallback strategy
- README with setup and usage
- screenshots or logs when relevant

### Minimum rubric threshold
- Meets in all criteria
- Exceeds in at least 2 criteria
- [Beginner Rubric](../../assessments/rubrics/beginner-rubric.md)
