## How to Read This Changelog

**Version Format:** `vMAJOR.MINOR.PATCH`

- **MAJOR** (v2.0) - Major additions or restructuring
- **MINOR** (v1.1) - New content, improvements, additions
- **PATCH** (v1.0.1) - Bug fixes, clarifications, small updates

**Entry Format:**
- **Added** - New content or features
- **Improved** - Better explanations or organization
- **Fixed** - Bug fixes or corrections
- **Changed** - Modifications to existing content
