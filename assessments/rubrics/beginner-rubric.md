# Beginner Rubric

Use this rubric to evaluate beginner submissions across languages.

## Criteria
- Core correctness
- Readability and clarity
- Basic testing or validation
- Documentation completeness
- Delivery readiness

## Rating scale
- Meets: requirement satisfied with no major gaps
- Exceeds: requirement exceeded with clear evidence

## Minimum threshold
- Meets in all criteria
- Exceeds in at least 2 criteria
