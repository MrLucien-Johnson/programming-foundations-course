# Advanced Rubric

Use this rubric to evaluate advanced submissions across languages.

## Criteria
- Architecture and scalability
- Reliability and observability
- Security and risk management
- Performance and efficiency
- Documentation and handoff quality

## Rating scale
- Meets: requirement satisfied with no major gaps
- Exceeds: requirement exceeded with clear evidence

## Minimum threshold
- Meets in all criteria
- Exceeds in at least 2 criteria
